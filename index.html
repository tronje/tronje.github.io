Bio

My name's Tronje, I'm 22 years old, and I love programming, and computer science in general. I'm always looking for interesting new challenges, and if you're looking for someone who values clean, readable and efficient code, as well as good communication and teamwork, feel free to get in touch!

I've explored quite a few different programming languages; I'd say I'm most comfortable and efficient with Python. Though, at the moment, the language I'm having the most fun with is Rust. I don't mind terribly which language is used for a project, as long as it isn't PHP. Or COBOL, I guess.

My OS of choice is Linux. At home I use Arch Linux for everything; I'm comfortable navigating the Linux userland, working on a Linux system, and deploying software on one.

In my freetime I cook with a passion, write sometimes silly, sometimes cool code, and pretend like I'm a movie critic.
