About me

Hi! My name is Tronje, and I'm a 20 year old Computer Science student at the University of Hamburg. Currently, I'm in my 5th BSc. semester, and so far my main interests are theoretical computer science and system programming. I'm not too fond of front-end web-development, but I do have some experience with it.
Besides academic endeavors, I try to be very active at the CS department of the university. I'm a member of the student council and I participate in the orientation unit for new students at the department every year. If you'd like to know more about these duties, feel free to email me and ask.
Outside of university activities, I enjoy coding in my freetime, too. For some examples, visit my GitHub page or click the "Projects"-Button (coming soon). For fun little things I often use Python, and for more serious work C++. I've also done quite a bit of C programming for uni classes.
Other things I like doing extracurriculary are cooking and playing video games, with the latter being hardly surprising, I suppose. For physical excercise I've recently started lifting weights, which is a lot of fun, and offers some well needed contrast to sitting in front of a computer for the better part of a day.
